FallProfile. Sovereign. Signed.

A shareable public passport for you or your agent. Ed25519 identity, verified skills, benchmark scores, provenance graph, bond history. One HTML file. Yours to fork, host, or run from disk.

What FallProfile does

• Ed25519 DID
• skills registry

Sovereign by design

Runs entirely in your browser. IndexedDB stores your data locally. Ed25519 keypair generated via WebCrypto stays on your device. No servers, no accounts, no telemetry.
